Short Stories by Enrique Anderson Imbert

I plan to translate the books of short stories by Enrique Anderson Imbert. He was born in Córdoba, Argentina in 1910. He graduated from the University of Buenos Aires and taught at the University of Tucumán. Later he came to the U. S. and taught at the university of Michigan, and at Harvard. He was known for his “microcuentos” which had a mixture of fantasy and magic realism. He died in 2000 in Buenos Aires. I will post the stories, as they are translated.
